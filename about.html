Our Mission & History

Our school's history dates back to 1995, when a group of visionary parents and educators came together to create a unique learning community in the heart of Austin. Today, we remain committed to our founding principles of academic excellence, character development, and community service.

At PDF Greater Austin Area Private Schools, we believe in a holistic approach to education that combines academic rigor with creativity, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students and foster a love of learning.

From sports teams to clubs and service organizations, our students have plenty of opportunities to develop their passions and build lasting friendships. Our safe and welcoming campus is the perfect place for your child to explore their interests and make lifelong connections.
