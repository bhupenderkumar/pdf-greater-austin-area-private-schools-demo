Our Mission & History

Founded in 1995, PDF Greater Austin Area Private Schools has a rich history of providing high-quality education in the heart of Austin. Our school was established on the principles of academic excellence, character development, and community engagement.

At PDF Greater Austin Area Private Schools, we believe that every student learns differently. Our teaching approach combines traditional instruction with innovative methods, ensuring that each child receives a tailored education that meets their unique needs.

Beyond academics, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities and character-building programs that foster social responsibility, teamwork, and emotional intelligence. Our students participate in sports, music, art, and community service, developing essential life skills and a sense of purpose.
