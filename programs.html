Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program provides a nurturing environment for young learners, introducing them to essential skills like literacy, numeracy, and socialization. Our experienced teachers use play-based instruction to foster a love of learning and prepare students for kindergarten.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students build on foundational skills, developing a strong foundation in reading, writing, and mathematics. Our teachers use innovative methods to engage students and promote critical thinking and problem-solving.

📊

Middle/Upper

In our Middle and Upper School programs, students explore advanced subjects like science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Our teachers mentor students as they develop research skills, critical thinking, and effective communication.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program offers hands-on learning experiences in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Students engage in inquiry-based projects, developing problem-solving skills and creativity.

🎭

Arts

At PDF Greater Austin Area Private Schools, we believe that the arts are essential to a well-rounded education. Our Arts program offers courses in music, theater, and visual arts, fostering creativity, self-expression, and confidence.

🏈

Athletics

Our Athletics program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Students participate in a range of sports, developing essential skills and building a sense of community.
